Frequently Ask Questions

How do I register my school on the UPSSA Portal?

To register your school on the UPSSA Portal, you need to follow the following steps:

  1. Visit the UPSSA Portal on https://upssaonline.com
  2. Click on the Register school button
  3. Fill in the required details
  4. Click on the submit button
  5. You will receive a confirmation email with your login details
  6. Log in to the portal using the provided details
  7. Complete the registration process by confirming the required information
  8. Your school will be registered on the UPSSA Portal

How do I register athletes on the UPSSA Portal?

While you're logged in to the UPSSA Portal, you can register athletes by following these steps:

  1. Click on the Athletes tab
  2. Click on the Add Athlete button
  3. Fill in the required details
  4. Click on the submit button
  5. The athlete will be registered on the UPSSA Portal

How do I register officials on the UPSSA Portal?

To register officials on the UPSSA Portal, you need to follow the following steps:

  1. Click on the Officials tab
  2. Click on the Add Official button
  3. Fill in the required details
  4. Click on the submit button
  5. The official will be registered on the UPSSA Portal

How do I enroll for a Championship on the UPSSA Portal?

To enroll for a championship follow the following steps:

  1. Click on the Enrollments tab
  2. Click on the Enroll School button
  3. Fill in the required details
  4. Click on the submit button
  5. Open the school enrollment and click on the enroll athletes button
  6. Select the athletes you want to enroll
  7. Click on the submit button
  8. The athletes will be enrolled for the championship.

How do I get an Album for my team?

Go to Enrollments and click on the Generate Album button next to the championship.

How do I edit athlete Info incase of a mistake?

Write a letter to the UPSSA Secretariat requesting for the changes to be made. Make sure the letter is signed by the headteacher of the school and on a headed paper. email the letter to upssaportal@gmail.com
